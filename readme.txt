Add reviews from different review platforms to your website in minutes

== Description ==
Add reviews from different review platforms to your website in minutes, gather new reviews and testimonials from your customers and improve your website conversion rate by showing right reviews to the right user.

## Displaying reviews and testimonials
+50 ways of showing off your reviews and testimonials on your wordpress site.

## Importing reviews
You can import reviews from Facebook, Google, Trustpilot, G2, Capterra, Yelp, Tripadvisor and add them to your website.

## Gathering new reviews and testimonials
You can gather new reviews and testimonials from your customers with ease with our review gathering form.

## Improve website conversion rate
Improve your website conversion rate with the help of our A/B testing and personalization features.

## Installation:
1. Go to plugins > Add New
2. Search for "Trustmary"
3. Click "Install Now"

Or

1. Download the zip file
2. Upload the zip file via Plugins > Add New > Upload
